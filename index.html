Who we are

Arfhacom is an IT consulting company led by engineers with around 15 years of experience across local and international environments. We focus on dependable delivery, practical solutions, and long-term reliability.

What we do

We cover IT helpdesk, networks and infrastructure, system and cloud administration, cybersecurity, IT training, and IT consultation—handled with care and professionalism.

How we work

We operate with integrity and respect, choosing projects that align with our values while serving clients with clarity and responsibility.

Services

IT Helpdesk & Support

We design and implement helpdesk solutions based on ITIL best practices. Our services cover ticketing systems, remote support, onboarding and offboarding workflows, knowledge base setup, and SLA management. This ensures faster response, structured processes, and a reliable support backbone for your business.

Networks & Infrastructure

We design and implement secure, scalable, and reliable networks from the ground up. Our services include structured cabling, rack and topology planning, switch/router/firewall configuration, and full network solutions from local connectivity to site-to-site integration. With proper monitoring and documentation, we deliver an infrastructure that supports long-term business growth.

System & Cloud Administration

We take care of your servers, applications, and cloud platforms to keep everything stable and secure. From managing users, updates, and backups to handling deployments, monitoring, and automation, we ensure your IT environment runs smoothly and adapts to your business needs.

Cybersecurity

We focus on strengthening defenses and ensuring your business is ready for modern threats. Our services include system hardening, vulnerability assessments, endpoint protection, and monitoring. We help organizations build CSIRT functions and Incident Response plans using the NIST framework, supported with tabletop exercise (TTX) scenarios. We can also help you establish a Security Operations Center (SOC) to improve detection, response, and long-term security resilience.

IT Training

We provide structured training programs covering IT fundamentals, IT helpdesk and support, and defensive security practices, including security awareness for all employees. Our sessions are designed to be practical and easy to follow, helping participants build skills step by step. With our teaching experience, we make sure every program delivers value and prepares teams to handle real IT challenges with confidence.

IT Consultation

We help you plan, assess, and improve your IT environment with solutions tailored to your goals. Want to explore more? Contact us or drop us a message — let’s discuss what you need!
